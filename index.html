K0IRO DMR Repeater Information

Our DMR repeater is now fully online and available for use. We are excited to bring expanded digital voice capabilities to the Iowa Radio Operators community and look forward to its continued growth and activity on the air.

The repeater is fully integrated with our monitoring tools. Operators can view live activity, callsigns, talkgroups, signal quality, and system performance through our Digital Last Heard dashboard. This provides excellent visibility into repeater usage and helps operators understand current traffic and system behavior.
